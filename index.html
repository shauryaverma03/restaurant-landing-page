Our Story

Where Every Meal
Tells a Story

Born from a love of honest cooking and community, The Hearthstone Kitchen & Bar has been Brooklyn's beloved neighborhood gathering place since 2010. We partner with local farms and artisans to bring you dishes that celebrate the season — crafted with care, served with warmth. From our hand-pulled pastas to our signature craft cocktails, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home.
